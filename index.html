Health for All Partnership

Health for All Partnership is a nursing student-led, faculty-supervised health clinic. We seek to bridge the gap between health care and social services for clients otherwise at risk of falling through the cracks. Our clinics are focused on providing primary health care, mental health support, health education, and advocacy; as adapted to meet the community needs of each partner site.
